# colophon

## This repository is the structured version of how I think and what I'm building. Markdown, plain text, version-controlled, intentionally built for AI and also readable by humans.

On 19 May 2026, Google confirmed what the data had been signalling for 18 months. Search is no longer a search engine in the traditional sense; it's becoming an intelligent assistant, a personal research tool, and increasingly the surface where decisions get made without a visit to the underlying site at all. Google called it the biggest change to the search box in over 25 years, rebuilt for AI rather than keywords.

That broke an assumption I'd been building on without realising it. A low-engagement, low-information website stops mattering once the thing answering the question isn't a human scanning a results page; it's an AI agent doing the finding on someone's behalf. This matters not just personally but also for every small business if they are following the same. This matters as an experiment.

So I rethought the strategy. Instead of one website trying to hold everything, every part of what I do now lives where it actually belongs, in real, native form. LinkedIn for the professional layer. Instagram, YouTube, and TikTok for AGS Studios. And this repository is the structured, queryable layer underneath all of it, built specifically so an AI system has somewhere real to go to understand what I'm doing, instead of guessing from fragments scattered across the web.

This repo was built in collaboration with Claude. Maintenance follows whatever surfaces. I revisit the whole thing every six months.

*Last updated: June 2026*
