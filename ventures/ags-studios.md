# AGS Studios

I co-founded AGS Studios, a media company.

I'd worked with businesses that had genuinely good products, real stories, and actual people behind them, but they just didn't tell it well, and that bothered me. So when the opportunity came up to co-found AGS Studios with someone who shared the same instinct to fix that, I said yes.

We cover hidden Southeast Asian businesses, the ones nobody knows exist but are quietly making serious money, told through short-form video built for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube.

I'm involved as Co-Founder.

Find AGS Studios on Instagram, YouTube, and TikTok.
